# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

EULens is an educational project dedicated to making European Parliament data more accessible and understandable. We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone who contributes to or uses this project.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- **Be respectful**: Treat everyone with dignity, regardless of their background, identity, or level of experience
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and assume good intentions from others
- **Be collaborative**: Work together toward improving transparency and understanding of EU legislative processes
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and help them get oriented with the project
- **Be professional**: Keep discussions focused on the project and avoid personal attacks or harassment

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics or identity
- Deliberate disruption of project discussions or activities
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional or academic setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project communications (email, chat, forums)
- Public representations of the project

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainer at nic.lorusso@icloud.com. All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

Violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from the project.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct reflects our commitment to maintaining EULens as an open, educational resource focused on civic engagement and democratic transparency.
